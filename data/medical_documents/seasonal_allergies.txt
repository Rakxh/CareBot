# Seasonal Allergies (Allergic Rhinitis)

## Overview
Seasonal allergies occur when the immune system overreacts to airborne substances such as pollen, leading to inflammation of the nasal passages.

## Common Symptoms
- Sneezing
- Runny or stuffy nose
- Itchy or watery eyes
- Postnasal drip
- Fatigue

## Causes
- Pollen from trees, grasses, and weeds
- Mold spores
- Genetic predisposition to allergies

## Treatment Options
- Antihistamines
- Nasal corticosteroid sprays
- Decongestants for short-term relief
- Allergen immunotherapy for persistent cases

## Prevention Tips
- Checking pollen forecasts and limiting outdoor exposure
- Keeping windows closed during high pollen counts
- Showering after outdoor activity
- Using air filters indoors

## When to See a Doctor
Consult a licensed healthcare provider if symptoms of seasonal allergies (allergic rhinitis) are severe, persistent, or worsening, or if you are uncertain about the right course of action.
